Introduction

LoPop

LoPop is an art shop located in Santa Monica where local artists have the opportunity build their careers by easily sell their work to local customers. It’s a great way for local artists to get their careers started and for customers to support the local community. LoPop is in the retail business.

Target audience

LoPop’s target audience artist who live in or around Santa Monica looking for a way to get their careers started and build their name/reputation as an artist. It’s target audience also includes those who live in or around Santa Monica that want an accessible way to support those in the community.

These artists' primary goal is to get exposure for their art work and build their reputation as artists. For those buying these artists' art, their goal is to find unique local art and support artists in Santa Monica community.

About

It can be difficult for artists and creators to get their careers started, which is why our founders created LoPop in 2016. Our goal is to give local artists bigger platform to sell their work from to get their careers started. We also strive to make supporting local artists easily accessible to those in the Santa Monica community. LoPop offers local Santa Monica artists visibility in the community and an easy way for the community to support these artists. We give artists to sell a wide variety of pieces. These pieces range from sculptures to jewelry to clothing/garments to paintings to candles and much more!
